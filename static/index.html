About DocDrop

Built because booking a doctor shouldn’t be hard.

DocDrop started in 2024 out of a simple frustration — why does booking a doctor still involve 10-minute hold music and a callback from someone who doesn’t know your name?

We built a platform that puts patients first. Pick a specialist, see their actual availability, pay a flat fee, and walk in. No insurance maze, no front-desk friction, no surprises.

We’re currently operating across Mumbai and Pune with six verified specialists, and growing fast.
